**Tribe**
- id_tribe: SERIAL (PK)
- name: VARCHAR(100) UNIQUE
- founding_date: DATE
- max_members: INTEGER CHECK (max_members > 0)

**Player**
- id_player: SERIAL (PK)
- username: VARCHAR(50) UNIQUE
- level: INTEGER CHECK (level BETWEEN 1 AND 100)
- join_date: DATE
- id_tribe: INTEGER (FK, NULL)

**Creature**
- id_creature: SERIAL (PK)
- nickname: VARCHAR(100)
- level: INTEGER CHECK (level BETWEEN 1 AND 150)
- sex: CHAR(1) CHECK (sex IN ('M', 'F'))
- status: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (status IN ('wild', 'tamed', 'dead'))
- health: NUMERIC(8,2)
- stamina: NUMERIC(8,2)
- id_specie: INTEGER (FK)
- id_player: INTEGER (FK, NULL)

**Specie**
- id_specie: SERIAL (PK)
- name: VARCHAR(100) UNIQUE
- diet: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (diet IN ('carnivore', 'herbivore', 'omnivore', 'piscivore'))
- threat_level: INTEGER CHECK (threat_level BETWEEN 1 AND 5)
- can_fly: BOOLEAN
- can_swim: BOOLEAN

**Map**
- id_map: SERIAL (PK)
- name: VARCHAR(100) UNIQUE
- biome: VARCHAR(30) CHECK (biome IN ('jungle', 'arctic', 'desert', 'ocean', 'volcanic'))
- difficulty: INTEGER CHECK (difficulty BETWEEN 1 AND 5)

**Mutation**
- id_mutation: SERIAL (PK)
- attribute: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (attribute IN ('health', 'stamina', 'melee', 'speed', 'weight'))
- description: TEXT

**Item**
- id_item: SERIAL (PK)
- name: VARCHAR(100) UNIQUE
- type: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (type IN ('kibble', 'narcotic', 'food', 'tool'))
- rarity: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (rarity IN ('common', 'uncommon', 'rare', 'legendary'))

---

**Entidades associativas**

**Domestication**
- id_domestication: SERIAL (PK)
- id_player: INTEGER (FK)
- id_creature: INTEGER (FK)
- tame_date: DATE
- method: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (method IN ('passive', 'violent'))
- effectiveness: NUMERIC(5,2) CHECK (effectiveness BETWEEN 0 AND 100)
- time_spent_min: INTEGER

**Creature_mutation**
- id_creature: INTEGER (FK)
- id_mutation: INTEGER (FK)
- generation: INTEGER CHECK (generation >= 1)
- value: NUMERIC(8,2)

**Spawn_rate**
- id_specie: INTEGER (FK)
- id_map: INTEGER (FK)
- frequency: VARCHAR(20) CHECK (frequency IN ('rare', 'uncommon', 'common'))

**Inventory**
- id_player: INTEGER (FK)
- id_item: INTEGER (FK)
- quantity: INTEGER CHECK (quantity > 0)
